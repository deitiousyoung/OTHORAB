Conditions We Treat

From foot conditions to neurological rehabilitation — we cover 64+ orthopedic conditions with expert care and modern devices.

🦶 Foot & Ankle

Flat Foot (Pes Planus)

A condition where the arch of the foot collapses, causing the entire sole to touch the ground. Can cause pain and affect walking.

Common

Plantar Fasciitis

Inflammation of the plantar fascia causing heel pain, especially in the morning. One of the most common foot complaints.

Common

Clubfoot (Talipes)

A congenital deformity where the foot is twisted inward and downward. Treated with casting, bracing or surgery.

Congenital

Heel Spur

A bony outgrowth on the heel bone often associated with plantar fasciitis. Causes sharp pain when walking or standing.

Bone

Achilles Tendinitis

Inflammation of the Achilles tendon causing pain at the back of the heel. Common in runners and active individuals.

Tendon

Bunion (Hallux Valgus)

A bony bump that forms on the joint at the base of the big toe. Causes pain and affects footwear choices.

Deformity

🦵 Knee

Knee Osteoarthritis

Degeneration of knee cartilage causing pain, stiffness and swelling. Most common in older adults and affects mobility.

Degenerative

ACL Injury

Tear or sprain of the anterior cruciate ligament. Common in sports involving sudden stops or changes in direction.

Sports

Knock Knee (Genu Valgum)

A condition where the knees angle inward. Can cause pain and difficulty walking if severe.

Deformity
